About Team Citrus

Team Citrus consists of people with the passion and experience to change the world of consumer payments in India. We aim to create a company that puts the consumer and their needs in the centre of everything we do. Our collective backgrounds are from the worlds of technology, user experience, payments, and banking. We have worked across the world in companies like Apple, First Data, ICICI Bank, Cisco, and Yahoo.

Our Investors

Sequoia Capital

History has witnessed Sequoia; one of the oldest and largest venture capital firms in the world. Sequoia has invested in the likes of Paypal, Stripe, Square, Google, WhatsApp, Micromax, Evernote and more. The support of such a successful, proven partner has only escalated our passion and determination.

Beenos

Beenos is a noteworthy Japanese investment giant with a strong foothold in emerging markets. Their wings spread across countries such as Indonesia, Singapore, Vietnam, Thailand, Turkey, India, Japan and the United States. They focus majority of their investments in the payments and marketplace domains.

E-Context Asia

E-Context Asia is a leading provider of online payment services and e-Commerce solutions across Asia. Their network is spread across, Shanghai, Jakarta, Hong Kong and Tokyo. They provide services through 8 subsidiaries located in Japan, including VeriTrans and ECONTEXT; two major online payment service providers in Japan.
